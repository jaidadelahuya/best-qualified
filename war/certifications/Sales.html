Inbound Sales Certification
  • The Inbound Sales Certification is a free qualification offered by Hubspot
  • It consists of a 5 self paced classes.
  • Can be taken by sales associates, sales leaders, entrepreneurs and inbound marketers.
  • created from 20 years of modeling, interviewing, and coaching the top 1% of sales professionals in the world
  • Inbound Sales Certificate can be earned by:
    • Signing up for a complimentary account or Logging in
    • Finding inbound Sales certification library and study guides
    • Taking the self-study classes
    • Passing the certification exam
  • A certified professional gets a personalised certificate or badge which can be printed out or displayed on a website, LinkedIn profile, email signature, etc.
  • Find more information on http://certification.hubspot.com/inbound-sales-certification
