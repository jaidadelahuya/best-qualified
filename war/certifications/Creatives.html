Creatives Certifications

ACA
  • Adobe Certified Associate is an entry-level certification aimed at demonstrating and validating communication skills using a particular digital communication tool
  • This is the certification for individuals getting ready for internships and graduate jobs
  • The areas covered by the ACA are as follows:
  • Visual Communication with Adobe Photoshop
  • Rich Media Communication with Flash
  • Web Communication with Dreamweaver
  • To prepare for the exams, an individual can use self-study materials made by publishers or materials available on Adobe website
  • Find more information on http://training.adobe.com/
ACE
  • Adobe Certified Expert (ACE)
  • The Adobe Certified Expert is a professional-level certification aimed at industry professionals who have a deep level of mastery in using Adobe technology
  • The Adobe Certified Expert is a qualified professional in web design, digital media, or rich internet application development
  • The ACE is offered for almost all Adobe products.To prepare for the exams, a professional can use books from Adobe Press and other publishers, self-paced online training from Lynda.com and other sources, and training from Adobe Authorised Training Centres
  • To earn the ACE if a professional already has the ACA:
  • Gain experience with daily use of the product
  • Self-assess your skills against the ACE objectives within the Prep Guide
  • If needed, find an intermediate-level course from an Adobe Authorized Training Center or Adobe Certified Instructor
  • Take the ACE exam once you are prepared
  • Find more information on http://training.adobe.com/
CIW
  • CIW Web Design Professional
  • CIW (Certified Internet Web Professional) is a vendor-neutral certification series for Information Technology within the United States
  • For the Web design professional, it is important to have a working knowledge of both Web design and e-commerce. Therefore, two separate certification exams must be passed, namely:
  • CIW Web Design Specialist
  • CIW E-commerce specialist
  • This certification is ideal for web and graphic designers, web marketing professionals, creative directors, art directors, advertising professionals
  • Find more information on http://www.ciwcertified.com
Hubspot Design Certification
  • HubSpot Design Certification is a free certification from HubSpot
  • It is built to test knowledge and proficiency of a Designer on HubSpot's design tools
  • The Designer is expected to create styled templates that help HubSpot customers hit their marketing goals with responsive websites
  • The certification is intended for web designers who have, at minimum, an intermediate familiarity with HTML and CSS. Although only a basic knowledge of HTML is required for building site templates on HubSpot
  • HubSpot Design Certification can be earned by:
  • Creating a free developer account or Logging in
  • Finding HubSpot Design certification library and study guides
  • Taking the self-study classes
  • Passing the certification exam
  • A certified professional gets a personalised certificate or badge which can be printed out or displayed on a website, LinkedIn profile, email signature, etc. Find more information on http://certification.hubspot.com/inbound-sales-certification
