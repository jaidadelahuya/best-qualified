Advertising Certifications

APCON
  • Advertising Practitioners Council of Nigeria (APCON) 
  • APCON offers membership and professional examinations
  • 3 part syllabus
  • Only candidates who possess a Degree or Higher National Diploma in any discipline are eligible can enroll in the programme
  • enroll initially in the part one and progress to parts two and three subsequently
  • Candidates with Degree/HND is in Advertising, Mass Communications, Marketing or Graphics Arts will be exempted from courses they have passed in their Degree/HND programmes on presentation of valid academic transcripts
  • Successful completion of the 3 Parts of the examinations qualifies a candidate for
  • the APCON Professional Diploma in Advertising
  • for registration as Associate Member of the Advertising Profession
  • to lawfully practice advertising in Nigeria
  • Examinations are conducted twice a year - May/June and Nov/Dec
IAB
  • Interactive Advertising Bureau
  • Offer IAB Digital Ad Operations Certification 
  • IAB Digital Ad Operations Certification is available worldwide and to anyone working in digital ad operations at Advertising Agencies, Digital Publishers, Trading Desks, Demand and Supply-Side Platforms, Exchanges and Brands organizations
    • Criteria
    • A minimum of two (2) years of work experience in Digital Advertising Operations
    • It can also include operational roles in the companies
    • Eligible candidates will also be required to submit a signed Code of Ethics that all certified individuals must abide by and uphold
    • Recertification is required 
TAS
  • Provided by the PPA (Promotional Products Association International
  • the world's largest and oldest international not-for-profit promotional products association. 
  • Trained Advertising Specialist (TAS )
  • TAS Certificate is a PPAI Professional Development initiative aimed at providing promotional products professionals with less than 3 years of industry experience an avenue for earning an industry credential 
  • Levels of the TAS are:
  • Certified Advertising Specialist (CAS )
  • Master Advertising Specialist (MAS )
  • Master Advertising Specialist Plus (MAS +)
  • Recertification is compulsory 
  • every three years
  • recertification points may be earned through attendance at live industry classes, webinars, PPAI accredited Regional Association education and with approved third-party education
  • Membership with PPAI is not a requirement to renew your PPAI Certification
  • Find more information on www.ppai.org
