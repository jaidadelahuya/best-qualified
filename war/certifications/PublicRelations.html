PR Certifications

AMA

The Nigerian Institute for Public Relations (NIPR) Professional body of qualified Public Relations Practitioners in Nigeria The NIPR offers membership, trainings and qualifications. The qualifications consist of 2 levels Professional Certificate in Public Relations Professional Diploma in Public Relations For requirements for each certificate and a list of the modules involved, go to http://nipr-ng.org/
