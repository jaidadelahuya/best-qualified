PR Certifications

NIPR
  • The Nigerian Institute for Public Relations (NIPR)
  • Professional body of qualified Public Relations Practitioners in Nigeria
  • The NIPR offers membership, trainings and qualifications
  • The qualifications consist of 2 levels
  • Professional Certificate in Public Relations
  • Professional Diploma in Public Relations
  • For requirements for each certificate and a list of the modules involved, go to http://nipr-ng.org/
CIPR
  • Chartered Institute of Public Relations (CIPR)
  • In the UK
  • Qualifications
  • Foundation Award in Public Relations
  • Advanced Certificate in Public Relations
  • Diploma in Public Relations
  • Internal Communication Certificate
  • Internal Communication Diploma
  • Crisis Communication Diploma
  • Public Affairs Diploma
  • Find more information on http://www.cipr.co.uk/content/qualifications
