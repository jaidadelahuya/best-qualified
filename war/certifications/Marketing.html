Marketing Certifications

NIMN
  • The National Institute of Marketing of Nigeria (NIMN)
  • The body for professionals engaged in marketing and related fields
  • Membership is compulsory for all marketing practitioners in Nigeria
  • The NIMN provides trainings, examinations and consultancy activities
  • The following are different certification levels:
    • Foundation Certificate in Marketing
    • Chartered Graduate Diploma in Marketing
    • Chartered Post Graduate Diploma in Marketing
  • For more information, visit http://nimn.com.ng/
AMA
  • The American Marketing Association (AMA)
  • Professional body based in the United States
  • The AMA provides membership, training, and certification in Marketing
  • To demonstrate a mastery of the core marketing knowledge and principles earn the Professional Certified Marketing (PCM)
  • PCM can be earned by:
    • providing proof of formal education and professional experience
    • successful completion of a certification exam
    • adherence to a code of ethics
    • The PCM requires recertification every 3 years
    • involves obtaining 40 Continuing Education Units (CEUs) over 3 years
    • Units can be obtained through seminars, eLearning, university coursework, instructor-led training, conferences and seminars, researching and publishing, speaking and instruction and other activities which encourage professional growth.
  • Find more information on http://www.ama.org/
CIM
  • Chartered Institute of Marketing (CIM)
  • Professional association based in the UK
  • The CIM offers membership, training and certification in Marketing and related subjects
  • Certification Levels are as follows:
    • Foundation Certificate in Marketing
    • Certificate in Professional Marketing
    • Diploma in Professional Marketing
    • Post Graduate Diploma in Marketing
  • Find more information on http://www.cim.co.uk/
CMMP
  • The Certified Marketing Management Professional (CMMP)
  • certification provided by the International Institute of Marketing Professional (IIMP)
  • The CMMP demonstrates high professional, educational and ethical standards in marketing
  • The CMMP consists of 4 levels:
    • Associate CMMP
    • Manager CMMP
    • Executive CMMP
    • Chartered CMMP
  • The CMMP can be earned by:
    • meeting specific academic requirements
    • meeting specific work experience requirements
    • going through certain mandatory courses
    • passing an exam demonstrating competencies in the workplace
    • successful completion of previous CMMP level where appropriate
  • Requirements vary with the different levels. For more information visit http://www.theiimp.org/cmmp-designation/
Inbound Marketing Certification
  • The inbound Certification is a free qualification offered by Hubspot
  • It covers the principal components of Inbound Marketing
  • Has self paced set of courses
  • The Inbound Marketing Certification Course can be taken by Marketers, non-marketers, entrepreneurs and students
  • Inbound Certificate can be earned by:
    • Signing up for a complimentary account or Logging in
    • Finding inbound Marketing certification library and study guides
    • Taking the self-study classes
    • Passing the certification exam
  • A certified professional gets a personalised certificate or badge which can be printed out or displayed on a website, LinkedIn profile, email signature, etc.
  • Find more information on http://certification.hubspot.com/inbound-certification
